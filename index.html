BLIND HONEY

Instructions

You are a nearly-blind bee in a quest to capture four flowers for your hive.
Use a, d, s, and w to control yourself, but be warned. Think fast and smart.
You can't see very well and will keep flying only until you see a rock or flower,
and if you don't act quick enough or fly off the screen,
you will be called back to your hive and lose.
